Definitely List

A list of words that I, personally, have a hard time pronouncing or writing. The title contains the word "Definitely" because it's a word I use quite frequently, however, I always forget the correct way of writing it, which frustrates me.

    Words I never remember the right way to write:

  • Definitely

    • (Adverb) Without doubt (used for emphasis).

  • Error

    • (Noun) A mistake; something that isn't right.

  • Despite

    • (Preposition) Without being affected by; in spite of.

  • Embarrassment

    • A feeling of self-consciousness, shame, or awkwardness.

  • Humiliating

    • (Adjective) Causing someone to feel ashamed and foolish by injuring their dignity and self-respect.

    Words I always forget the meaning or have a hard time explaining them:

  • Acknowledge

    • (Verb) Accept or admit the existence or truth of.

  • Accountability

    • (Noun) The fact or condition of being accountable; responsability.

  • Seldom

    • (Adverb) Not often; rarely.

  • Facetious

    • (Adjective) Trating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor; flippant.

  • Tabby

    • (Noun) a cat whose fur is mottled or streaked with dark stripes.
