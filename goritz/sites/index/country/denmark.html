Denmark




Location, Government and Religion


Denmark is a Parliamentary Monarchy, situated in Northern Europe and 75% of the population are registered members of the Evangelical Lutheran Church. Their capital and largest city is Copenhagen.

Prime Minister: Mette Frederiksen, is a Danish politician who has been Prime Minister of Denmark since June 2019. Queen of Denmark: Queen Margrethe II, is the head of state.




Population and Economy


Denmark's population is over 5,8 million. Their economy is a modern mixed one with comfortable living standards, a high level of government services and transfers, and a high dependence on foreign trade. Their monetary unit is the Danish Krone.

The Kingdom of Denmark has only one official language, Danish, the national language of the Danish people, but there are several minority languages spoken, namely Faroese, German, and Greenlandic.




Culture


Key values in a typical Danish family include punctuality, planning, and responsibility. They often trust and share their daily lives with each other. It is common for all family members to attend sports clubs or other local activities after work and school.




Curiosities


- One of the happiest countries in the world;

- The Danish flag is the oldest state flag still in use;

- If you’re unmarried at 25, you’ll get cinnamon thrown all over you at your birthday;

- The Danish language has no word for "please".
